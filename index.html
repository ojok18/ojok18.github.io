This is Kojo Asmah's work

I have created a website so you can see the work I have done as a Shades leader

So what IS Shades?

Shades is the Black and Latinx male affinity group at Taft. The goal of the affinity group is to create a space for Black or Latinx male students at Taft to find a space to connect with others of a similar identifier and give back to the community.

Learn more

General Involvement

As a Shades student leader I have planned events, participated in Taft hosted events to represent the affinity group, and helped students adjust to Taft. (The other leaders and I have also done game nights which are highlights for some students)

Learn more

Recent Work

My most recent involvement as a Shades leader was visiting Missouri for the SDLC, or Student Diversity Leadership Conference. I personally had a lot of fun and was able to meet people across the country of varied identifiers.
